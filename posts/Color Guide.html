Colors To Set Ourselves Apart

... kind of...

Having a thoughful color guide defining a consistent color scheme is helpful for a plethora of reasons but two important reasons are:

  1. Branding yourself
  2. Making sure your content is readable
Branding yourself is important to be recognizable. When someone goes to your website it should look like your website, there should be no confusing websites.

Making your content is readble allows visitors to your website to, well, read your content. You brought them to your website for one reason or another so make it easy for them to look around.
